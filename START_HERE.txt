FIC6 Torque Processor

How to use:

1. Double-click FIC6_Torque_Processor.exe.
2. Pick your FIC6 .txt log.
3. Pick your Torque .csv log.
4. Click the buttons from top to bottom:
   - Text to CSV
   - Sanitize Headers
   - Sanitize 2nd Step
   - Resample & Merge
   - RPM Offset Correction
   - AFR Analysis, Fuel Trim Analysis, or Interactive Plot

The app writes its output files into the same folder as the selected logs.

This is portable. The person using it does not need Python installed.
